How to Play

Objective

Survive the endless waves of alien invaders! Destroy enemies to increase your score, collect coins for upgrades, and grab power-ups to unleash your true potential.

Power-Ups

Bolt: Massively increases your rate of fire.

Shield: Protects you from a single enemy hit.

Magnet: Automatically pulls all nearby coins towards you.

Booster: Grants all power-ups at once for a short time!

Enemies

Scout: Weak but appears in large numbers.

Fighter: A tougher foe that fires back at you.

Cruiser: Slow and heavily armored. Takes many hits to destroy.

Hunter: Will actively track and chase your ship.

Hive Mother: The first boss! Spawns minions and is very durable.

The Crusher: The ultimate threat. Fast, powerful, and very dangerous.
